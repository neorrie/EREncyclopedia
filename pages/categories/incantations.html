Incantations

Incantations are a type of magic in Elden Ring that draw upon the power of faith. Used primarily for offensive spells, healing, buffs, and utility effects, incantations allow players to call upon divine or elemental forces. They require a Sacred Seal to cast and scale with the user's Faith stat, offering a versatile toolset for those who follow holy or primal paths.
