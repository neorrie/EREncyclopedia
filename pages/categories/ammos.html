Overview of Arrows and Bolts (Ammunition)

Arrows and Bolts serve as ammunition for ranged weapons in Elden Ring, such as bows, crossbows, and ballistae. Basic types like Beast Bone Arrows and Bolts can be crafted, while special ammunition is typically bought from merchants or found in limited quantities during exploration.

How Damage and Scaling Work

Arrows

Arrows add to your bow's total Attack Rating (AR) by combining their base values with the bow's. They do not scale independently, but they do benefit from the bow's scaling stats.

Bolts and Ballistae

Bolts and ballistae do not scale with any stats. Their damage remains fixed regardless of your character's build.

Scaling Breakdown by Damage Type

  • Physical AR → Scales with Strength and Dexterity
  • Lightning AR → Scales with Dexterity
  • Magic AR → Scales with Intelligence (only on the Horn Bow)
  • Fire & Holy AR → Scale with Faith (only on the Erdtree Bow and Erdtree Greatbow)
  • Status Effects (Bleed, Poison, Sleep) → Scale with Arcane (only on the Serpent Bow)
