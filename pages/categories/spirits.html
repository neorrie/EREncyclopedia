Spirits

Spirit Ashes in Elden Ring are summonable allies that assist you in battle, often taking the form of enemies or notable characters from the Lands Between. These companions are called forth using the Spirit Calling Bell and can provide support in combat situations, typically at the cost of Focus Points (FP)
