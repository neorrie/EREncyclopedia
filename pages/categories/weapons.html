Weapons

Weapons in Elden Ring come in a wide variety of types and styles, catering to different combat preferences and builds. They range from light, fast weapons like daggers and claws, which excel at quick attacks and critical hits, to heavy two-handed weapons such as colossal swords and greatswords that deliver powerful blows but require high strength.

Midrange options like spears, halberds, and whips offer a balance of reach and versatility. Weapons can be upgraded using Smithing Stones or Somber Smithing Stones depending on whether they are generic or unique.

Each weapon type has distinct attack patterns, scaling attributes, and damage types, allowing players to tailor their arsenal to their preferred playstyle and the challenges of the Lands Between
