Overview of Ashes of War

Ashes of War in Elden Ring are special items that allow players to customize their weapons by changing both their active skills and stat scaling. By equipping an Ash of War at a Site of Grace, you can replace a weapon's default skill with a new, unique ability, such as a powerful attack or defensive maneuver. Additionally, Ashes of War let you modify a weapon's affinity, altering how it scales with attributes like Strength, Dexterity, or Intelligence.

How to use Ashes of War

To use Ashes of War, follow these steps:

  1. Obtain the Whetstone Knife – This is found early in the game at the Gatefront Ruins. It’s required to apply Ashes of War.
  2. Rest at a Site of Grace – Choose the "Ashes of War" option in the menu.
  3. Select a Weapon – Pick a weapon that is compatible with the Ash of War you want to apply.
  4. Choose the Ash of War – Assign the new skill and select an affinity (e.g., Standard, Keen, Heavy, etc.), depending on what the Ash allows.
  5. Confirm the Change – Your weapon will now have the new skill and scaling based on the selected affinity.

Note: Special weapons (like boss weapons) usually cannot be customized with Ashes of War.
