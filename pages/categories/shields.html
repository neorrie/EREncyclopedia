Shields

Shields in Elden Ring are defensive equipment that come in a variety of forms, each tailored to different playstyles and needs. They are divided into three main categories: Small Shields, Medium Shields, and Greatshields.

Small Shields are lightweight and excel at parrying, making them ideal for agile characters who prioritize mobility over defense.

Medium Shields offer a balanced mix of protection and weight, suitable for those seeking reliable blocking without sacrificing too much speed.

Greatshields provide the highest damage negation and stability, perfect for players who focus on maximum defense, though they come with significant weight and strength requirements. Each shield type has its own strengths and trade-offs, allowing players to choose the best fit for their build, whether prioritizing blocking, parrying, or a combination of both
