Sorceries

Sorceries are a form of magic in Elden Ring that focus on intelligence-based spellcasting. Often associated with glintstone scholars and the Academy of Raya Lucaria, sorceries allow players to harness arcane energy for ranged attacks, crowd control, buffs, and utility effects. They require a staff to cast and scale primarily with the Intelligence stat, making them ideal for magic-focused builds.
