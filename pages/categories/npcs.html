About NPCs

NPCs in Elden Ring are the diverse characters that populate the Lands Between, each with their own stories, roles, and motivations. As players explore the world, they will meet a wide range of NPCs—some offer valuable lore that helps unravel the game’s mysteries, while others present unique questlines, act as merchants, or provide specialized services such as upgrading equipment or spirit ashes. Many NPCs are interconnected, with their narratives often overlapping or influencing each other, and their presence can significantly impact the player’s journey, from unlocking new items and magic to shaping the outcome of certain events
